About Kooky Walrus Treasures

Kooky Walrus Treasures was founded with a love for the ocean and all its wonders. We specialize in rare, whimsical, and ethically sourced deep-sea curiosities that spark adventure and imagination.

From seasoned divers to ocean dreamers, our shop offers unique treasures, including handcrafted nautical artifacts, bottled sea mist, and adventure maps leading to mystery finds. Every item has a story, and we invite you to dive in and discover yours.
