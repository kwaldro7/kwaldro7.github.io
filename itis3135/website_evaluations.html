Website Evaluations

Landbook

A website design inspiration gallery that is itself well-designed

  • Contrast: Uses an easy to read black and white color scheme throughout the entire website.
  • Repetition: Same button shapes at the top, as well as box shapes.
  • Alignment: A neat grid for the boxes is used with 4 columns throughout.
  • Proximity: Information about a box is displayed in close proximity.

ARNGREN.net

A website design with a variety of vehicles

  • Contrast: Too many colors competing with one another at the same time.
  • Repetition: There is no consistency in text size and color.
  • Alignment: The products are not aligned with one another, making the layout illogical.
  • Proximity: Some elements overlap others, making reading confusing.
