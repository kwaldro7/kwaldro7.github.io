Home

Archery is an ancient practice that combines precision, focus, and physical skill. It's both a competitive sport and a meditative hobby that people of all ages can enjoy.

The sport involves using a bow to shoot arrows at a target, testing accuracy and consistency. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, archery offers endless opportunities for growth and satisfaction.

About Me

I'm Keaton Waldron, an archery enthusiast with 2 years of experience. I started this hobby to meet new people my freshman year, and because I always wanted to try it.

My favorite aspect is the mental challenge - each shot requires complete concentration and control.
